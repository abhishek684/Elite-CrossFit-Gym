FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Get quick answers to common questions about visiting Elite CrossFit.

Do I need to book my first visit in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome during business hours, we recommend booking your first visit to ensure a coach is available for your movement assessment and facility tour. This also guarantees you a spot in your preferred trial class.

What if I've never done CrossFit before?

Perfect! Most of our members started as complete beginners. Our coaches specialize in teaching proper movement patterns from day one. Your trial class will be scaled to your current fitness level, and we offer a comprehensive 6-week foundations program for new members.

Is there parking available?

Yes! We have a free private parking lot with 50+ spaces. Additional street parking is available on Elite Street and surrounding blocks. The facility is also accessible via Metro Bus Route 42 and a 5-minute walk from Fitness City Metro Station.

What are your safety protocols?

Safety is our top priority. All coaches are certified in CPR/First Aid, we maintain comprehensive insurance coverage, and our equipment is inspected daily. We have clear emergency procedures, injury reporting protocols, and severe weather policies. Every new member receives a safety orientation.

Can I bring a friend to my trial class?

Absolutely! We encourage bringing friends and family. Each person will need to complete their own waiver and movement assessment, but you can participate in the same trial class together. We offer referral bonuses when friends join as members.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule?

No problem! You can cancel or reschedule your visit up to 2 hours before your scheduled time by calling us at (555) 123-ELITE or using our online booking system. We understand life happens and we're flexible with scheduling.
