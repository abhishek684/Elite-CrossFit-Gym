FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Get answers to common membership questions and concerns.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, all monthly memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Annual memberships have a 60-day cancellation policy. We also offer membership freezes for travel, injury, or other circumstances for up to 3 months per year.

What's included in membership fees?

All memberships include unlimited access to group classes, use of all equipment, shower facilities, and basic progress tracking. Higher tiers include personal training sessions, nutrition coaching, and advanced analytics as specified in each plan.

Do you offer trial periods?

Absolutely! We offer a comprehensive free week trial that includes a fitness assessment, goal consultation, and three coached classes. This gives you a complete experience of what Elite CrossFit offers before committing to a membership.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, you can change your membership tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. We'll prorate any differences to ensure fair billing.

Are there any additional fees?

No hidden fees! The only additional costs are optional services like specialized workshops, competition entry fees, or premium supplements. All core membership benefits are included in your monthly fee.

What if I'm injured or need to take time off?

We offer membership freezes for medical reasons, travel, or other circumstances. You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year with appropriate documentation. We also have modified workout programs for members recovering from injuries.
